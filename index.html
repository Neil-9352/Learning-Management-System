Welcome to the Learning Management System

Our Learning Management System (LMS) is a simple and efficient platform designed to facilitate online learning. It provides essential features for both teachers and students, ensuring a smooth and interactive learning experience.

Key Features:

  • 👩‍🏫 Teacher Registration & Login – Teachers can create an account, log in, and manage their courses.
  • 📂 Upload Study Materials – Teachers can upload lecture notes, PDFs, presentations, and other learning resources.
  • 📝 Create Quizzes – Teachers can design quizzes to assess students’ understanding of the subject.
  • 🎓 Student Registration & Login – Students can sign up with their course details and access learning materials.
  • 📖 View Study Materials – Students can browse and download course content shared by teachers.
  • ✅ Take Quizzes – Students can attempt quizzes and track their progress.
  • 🔒 Secure Authentication – Separate login systems for teachers and students ensure data privacy and security.
